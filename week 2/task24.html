Education

Education is the process of acquiring knowledge, skills, values, and habits through various means, including formal, informal, and non-formal settings. -> It encompasses different stages, from early childhood to higher education, and plays a crucial role in personal development, economic growth, social equity, and civic engagement. However, challenges such as access to quality education and adapting to rapid technological changes persist. -> Future trends include the integration of technology, personalized learning approaches, and an emphasis on lifelong education, highlighting its ongoing significance in shaping individuals and society.
