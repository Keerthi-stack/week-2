Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredient Amount
Spaghetti 200g
Ground beef 250g
Tomato sauce 200ml

Instructions

1. Boil the spaghetti.

2. Cook the ground beef until browned.

3. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 15 minutes.

4. Serve the sauce over spaghetti.
